Frequently Asked Questions

How do I split a bill among multiple people?
Enter your total bill amount, select the number of people, choose your tip percentage, and SplitTip Pro will automatically calculate each person's share including tip.
Can I use custom tip percentages?
Yes! While we provide common tip percentages (15%, 18%, 20%), you can enter any custom percentage or dollar amount that works for your situation.
Does the app require internet connection?
No, SplitTip Pro works completely offline. All calculations are performed locally on your device for maximum privacy and reliability.
Is my data secure and private?
Absolutely! We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information. Everything stays on your device. Check our Privacy Policy for full details.
What if I need to calculate tax separately?
You can either include tax in your total bill amount, or calculate the tip on the pre-tax amount - whichever method you prefer for your situation.

Troubleshooting

The app isn't calculating correctly
Please check that you've entered the bill amount and number of people correctly. If the issue persists, try restarting the app or contact our support team.
App crashes or won't open
Try force-closing the app and reopening it. If problems continue, restart your device. For persistent issues, please contact support with your device model and iOS version.
