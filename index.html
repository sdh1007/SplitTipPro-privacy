Privacy Promise: SplitTip Pro is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from our users. All calculations and data entry are processed locally on your device.

Information We Collect

SplitTip Pro operates with a zero-data-collection policy. Your privacy is our priority, and we've built our app to function entirely on your device without requiring any personal information.

Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (names, email addresses, phone numbers)
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking data
  • Financial information or transaction details
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs

How We Use Information

Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not use, process, or analyze any user data. All tip calculations, bill splitting functionality, and app preferences are handled entirely on your device without any external communication.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, trade, or transfer any information to third parties because we do not collect any information in the first place. Your calculations and usage patterns remain completely private to you.

Data Storage

Any data you enter into SplitTip Pro (such as bill amounts, tip percentages, or custom settings) is stored locally on your device only. This information never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third-party services.

Third-Party Services

SplitTip Pro operates as a standalone application and does not integrate with or use any third-party services that would collect or process your personal information. We maintain full control over our app's functionality to ensure your privacy.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect personal information, we comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar international privacy regulations.

Security

Since we do not collect or store any personal information on our servers, there is no risk of your personal data being compromised through our systems. All app functionality is contained within your device, providing the highest level of security for your privacy.
